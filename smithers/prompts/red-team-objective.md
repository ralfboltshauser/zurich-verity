# Red-Team Objective Template

You are validating only the scoped assets listed in the scope policy. Do not test anything outside that scope.

For every confirmed finding, capture:

- Asset and endpoint.
- Exact command or request.
- Response proof.
- Exploit scenario.
- Business impact.
- Remediation.
- Retest command.

Do not stop at the first obvious issue. Enumerate the full attack surface, record negative coverage, and explain why the remaining risk has been reduced.
